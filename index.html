Hello! I have developed many projects both individually and as a team. I like to identify problems and find solutions to these problems, and because I do watercolor and acrylic works in my life, I like to combine my projects with an artistic perspective. Among my projects are various Websites, Image Processing, Text Summarization, Data Hiding and Data Classification projects, Search Engine Optimization as an Intern, Chatbot and Mobile applications that I am the project manager of. I have served as both a board member and trainer in various communities. I am currently freelancing and developing my own web projects. In addition, I play volleyball and continue my painting works.

- Zehra Nur Kök
